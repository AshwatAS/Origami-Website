Interesting facts about Camel

  • Camels are called ships of the desert.
  • They store water in their body to quench their thirst.
  • Camels have long necks.

Interesting facts about Chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin colour is an important part of communication among chameleons.
  • Most chameleons have a prehensive tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

Interesting facts about Pigeon

  • Pigeons are gray and white in colour.
  • Pigeons can excrete waste in midair.
  • Pigeons are most commonly found birds.

Interesting facts about Teddy Bear

  • Teddy bears are the most common soft toys.
  • Kids usually aged from 3-5 years entertain themselves with teddy bears.
  • A teddy bear is usually brown in colour.

Interesting facts about Panda

  • Pandas live in bamboo jungles.
  • They are considered as wild animals.
  • Pandas are black and white in colour and they only eat bamboo.
